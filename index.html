Rocket Scientist Nambi Narayanan

Indian scientist

Here's a time line of Nambi Narayanan:

  • Early Life
    • Birth of Nambi Narayanan December 12, 1941- S. Nambi Narayanan was born at Nagercoil.
    • Schooling 1944- DVD Higher Secondary School, Nagercoil.
    • College - Mechanical Engineering 1959- Thiagarajar College of Engineering.
    • Master's degree in liquid propulsion 1969- Received NASA fellowship and was into Princeton university New Jersey.
  • Career
    • ISRO Technical Assistant 1966- At Thumba Equatorial Rocket launching station.
    • Invention 1970-Liquid fuel rocket technology.
    • First Engine - VIKAS- Tested Successfully.
  • Problems and later proved innocent
    • False Spy Case November 30, 1994- Nambi Narayanan, director of cryogenic project at ISRO was arrested.
    • Charges dismissed by CBI April 1996- - All charges against him was dismissed .
    • Supreme Court Order 1998- Proved Innocent by Supreme Court of India.
